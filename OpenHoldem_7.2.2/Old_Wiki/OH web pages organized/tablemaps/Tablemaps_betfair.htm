[edit] Betfair Poker

[edit] Status

Considerably stable with some issues for certain type of situations. Alexander Wang Shoes See the "Known Issues" section for details.

[edit] Instructions

  • Make sure that you have the newest version of the Betfair Poker software.
  • Launch the Betfair Poker - "Fixed Size" mode instead of Betfair Poker mode in which you can stretch the windows. (Got it? Launch the NON streatchable mode!). If the software launches the stretchable mode by default, you are screwed.

  • Make sure that the 4 color deck and Show Hand Strength modes are OFF.

  • MINIMIZE the "CONTROL PANEL" window totally so that even the "Autopost Blinds" and "Sit Out Next Hand" checkboxes are invisible. Othervise OH won't be able to read player3's bet.

[edit] Known Issues

  • Buttons have moved and are not detected any more.
  • In low stakes (from 5c/10c to 50c/1$), the chips may mess up with player 3, 4 and 5 bets in a way that they can't be read correctly.
  • Antes aren't read in any of the maps.
