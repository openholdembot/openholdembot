[edit] Title Text

s$titletext - A string always present in title bars of the windows you want to attach to. This is the part of the title bar text that uniquely identifies the correct table window.


Information you could extract from title bar text, thereby eliminating the need for the corresponding region:

r$c0istournament

r$c0smallblind

r$c0bigblind

r$c0bigbet

r$c0ante


[edit] Cards

WH Reference

How-to


Collect 52 images and calculate hash for each. If your table include scaled hole cards hash them adding an "s" (for Small) after the hash name.

i$Td - 10 of diamonds image (not scaled)

i$Tds - 10 of diamonds image (scaled)

h0$Td - 10 of diamonds hash0

h0$Tds - 10 of diamonds hash0 (scaled)

Card backs may also be used:

i$cardback - card back image

h0$cardback - card back image hash0

[edit] Players

For each seat define the following regions (where X is the seat):

r$pXseated

r$pXname

r$pXdealer

r$pXcardface1 or r$pXcardface1rank and r$pXcardface1suit

r$pXcardface0 or r$pXcardface0rank and r$pXcardface0suit

r$pXcardback

r$pXbet

r$pXbalance

r$pXactive

In addition, you can define the fowwowing optional regions:

r$uXseated

r$uXname

r$uXbalance

r$uXactive
