I bought Winholdem almost a year ago. I had never seen code other than HTML, and my knowledge was basic in that. I also was not a very good poker player. I had decent skills, but just inhale, and you could smell the fishiness. I was usually not able to spot the sucker at the table. But I would win enough, often enough to keep me addicted to the game.

When I first bought Winholdem, I expected that it would win right out of the box. I put him on some play money tables, and sure enough he won.

So of course my next move was to immediately make a deposit, and sit him at the $1 tables, because we were soon going to be rich!..

A couple of days later, as I am sure you can guess I was sadly disappointed.

I went frantically searching through the forums, and immediately found comfort and solace in the hundreds of "I BOUGHT WINHOLDEM AND IT DOESNT WIN MONEY" noobie posts.

And then I read the short, ridiculing responses to these posts by some of the veteran members.

I started copying formula after formula from the forums into my bots, and over and over I kept being disappointed.

Then it occured to me, it was profound, it was monumental, angels sang from the heavens.. I was just going to have to learn to code my own bot.

So I stopped bitching about how I got duped into buying this worthless bot that plays poker like a semi-retarded monkey, and began the task of studying....

I read poker books. I read just about this entire forum. I read the help files, release notes, and tutorials provided by support I created a library of code taken from this site, and categorized it I lost a lot of money, and a lot of time I didnt give up

No, I will not sell the bot, because the price would be too high to match my future earnings. (100/200 is the future target) But for now its on the the 1/2 tables.
