OpenHoldem Table Map

Requirements for Poker Academy Prospector

- Windows 2000 or Windows XP using the Windows Classic style appearance
- 4 color cards - check "Pause on hero"

This table map will "autoplay" the hand replayer included in the analisys software. The replayer will pause on hero, and OH will press the play button to continue the action. Very useful to review hands really played by your bot and see if/why some actions have not been performed as expected, or to debug your bot identifying unexpected decisions and why they happen.
