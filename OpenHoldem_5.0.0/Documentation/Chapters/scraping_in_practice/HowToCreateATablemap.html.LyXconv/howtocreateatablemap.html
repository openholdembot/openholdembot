My next step is to create the regions "pXbet", but the picture below shows an occlusion of "bets" between the players2 and player3.

Scraping the bet-amounts??? is the preferred solution, as it is easy and doesn’t utilize the CPU much. Scraping bet-amounts??? is not applicable in our case, as the numbers can be occluded by the chip-stacks. Therefore we have to use the alternative "chip scraping", i.e. recognizing and counting chip-stacks.

We will use the regions pXchip00, pXchip01 and pXchip10 for players, and c0potXchip00, c0potXchip01, c0potXchip10 for pots.
