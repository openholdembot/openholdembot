PokerTracker Symbols

OpenHoldem supports PokerTracker symbols, and so does OpenPPL. The development team did already create some symbols of general use that get shipped with the library. However: the possibilities are endless and you can for sure imagine other symbols that are useful. If you want to increase the power of OpenPPL please have a look at the chapter “Building Symbols On Your Own” and at Openholdems manual. It is not that difficult and if you want to contribute to the project your symbols could even become part of the next version.

Note: The following symbols need a working connection to Poker Tracker Database. Number of hands symbols are needed to weight reliability of the symbol you are interested in. Infact for some symbol 50 hand could perfectly represent an Opponent playing style while for other symbols you could be in the need of hundred or thousand hands.

Symbol

Meaning

PT_BigBlind_FoldBBToSteal

Big Blind fold percentage to steal-raises from late position or from the small blind

PT_BigBlind_Hands

Big Blind fold percentage to steal-raises from late positions

PT_LastCaller_VPIP

Last caller percentange of voluntary played hands

PT_LastRaiser_AttemptToStealBlinds

Percentage of steal-raises when first-in and in late position or in the small-blind

PT_LastRaiser_Hands

Last Raiser player number of hands stored in PT database

PT_LastRaiser_PFR

Preflop raising percentage of the last raiser

PT_LastRaiser_VPIP

Percentage of volutary played hands by the last raiser

PT_SmallBlind_FoldSBToSteal

Small Blind fold percentage to steal-raises from late positions

PT_SmallBlind_Hands

Player in Small Blind number of hands stored in PT database

PT_SmallBlind_VPIP

Percentage of voluntary played hands by the small blind
