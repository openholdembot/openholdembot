Handlists

OpenHoldem supports preflop handlists to simplify preflop coding. And the OpenPPL-converter supports them of course too, but with a slightly more verbose syntax. All you need is another custom section in your code:

CUSTOM 
 
LISTS 
// Your list definitions belong here 
 
PREFLOP 
...

and then you can define your lists, for example like that.

NEW LIST 007 
    // Allin-range against crazy maniacs 
    AA KK QQ JJ TT  // Pairs 
    AKs AQs KQs     // Suited hands 
    AKo AQo         // Offsuited hands 
END LIST

Nothing to care about, except 2 points:

  • the list number has to be in the range 0..999.
  • 007 and 7 are technically identical, however we recommend to always use 3 digits for readability and consistency.

After that you can use OpenHoldems islist-symbol like that

WHEN (Opponents = 1 AND ManiacFourBetsMe AND islist007) RAISEMAX FORCE

You might consider to comment your lists, because numbered lists have one small disadvantage: they are not very verbose.
