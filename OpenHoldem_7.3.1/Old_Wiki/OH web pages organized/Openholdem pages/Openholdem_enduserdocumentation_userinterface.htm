[edit] Status Bar

9 Positions (Still work in progress)

1. Bot is ready to play.
2. Your Hand
3. What's on the board
4. What do you have?
5. Hand strength out of the 169 or 1326 or 2652 possible combinations.
6. Value of prwin/prtie/prlos these are explained here [1]
7. Value of f$P, or Number of opponents. The value is accurate of what the bot knows.
8. NIT Value
9. What is the bot planning to do next? or Decision engine

[edit] fckra

Source(s):fckra

When these icons are illuminated ,

  • F - Fold button is recognised.
  • C - Call button is recognised.
  • K - Check button is recognised.
  • R - Bet or Raise buttons are recognised.
  • A - Allin button or swag entry field recognised.
