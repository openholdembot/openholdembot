0.1. Converting Table Maps

During the course of time both OpenScrape and the tablemap-format got improved. Especially tablemaps for version 1.x and 2.x differ significantly, so it is recommended to convert old tablemaps to the newest format. To do so it is usually sufficient to

  1. open the TM with the newest verion of OpenScrape
  2. save it again
  3. review, if it works
